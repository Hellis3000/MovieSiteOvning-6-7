The Iron Claw is an upcoming biographical sports film based on the life of professional wrestler Kevin Von Erich and the Von Erich family.

Premier Date: December 22, 2023

Director: Sean Durkin

Starring:

  • Zac Efron as Kevin Von Erich

  • Jeremy Allen White as Kerry Von Erich

  • Harris Dickinson as David Von Erich

Also starring: Maura Tierney, Stanley Simons, Holt McCallany, Lily James
